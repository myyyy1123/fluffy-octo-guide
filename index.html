Teen Rescued After Storm at Sea

Lara Moore, 18, was caught in a sudden storm while sailing. Knocked unconscious by her boat during the storm, she later awoke stranded and soaked.

Despite the chaos, she managed to call her mother—22 km away—after waiting for her wet map to dry. With only 9% battery, she shared her location and awaited rescue.

"I was waiting for what seemed like hours in the middle of the sea," Lara said. "I was about to give up hope when I heard the sound of a helicopter."

She was hospitalized with a concussion but expected to recover fully. The rescue team reminded sailors not to go off-piste due to rising storm risks. “Lara had a lucky escape,” they said.
